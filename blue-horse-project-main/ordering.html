How to Place Your Order for Selines

We're excited for you to try the amazing flavors of Selines! To streamline your ordering process, we've created a simple and convenient method using Google Forms. Follow these easy steps to order:

  1. Access the Order Form:

    Click on this Order Form when you are ready to place your order.

  2. Provide Contact Information:

    Enter your name, phone number, and email address so we can reach you with important updates and confirmations about your order.

  3. Select the Dishes You Want:

    Explore our menu on the form or on our website, featuring a variety of culinary delights crafted just for you. Click on the dishes you want to order.

  4. Select the Quantity of Each Dishes:

    Type in the number of orders per dish that you want.

  5. Choose Pickup Time:

    Select the preferred pickup time slot from the available options. Please note that pickup is only available during these designated hours for the listed specific date.

  6. Review and Submit Your Order Form:

    Please double-check the order details to make sure that everything you've selected is accurate. Once finished, click the "Submit" button at the bottom of the form to send in the order.

  7. Check for Confirmation Email:

    Shortly after submitting your order, you will receive a confirmation email with a copy of your order form and any additional information you may need.

  8. Payment Information:

    Follow the instructions in the confirmation email to make payment through Zelle, Cashapp, or Venmo. A confirmation note of the payment will be sent out when the payment is received and your order will be prepared.
